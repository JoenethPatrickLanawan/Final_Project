About Me & The Site

Who Am I

I’m Joeneth Patrick Lanawan (JP Lanawan), a mobile/android user with a basic skill in HTML/CSS and someone who's tired of sugarcoating reviews on various android games. I’ve been playing on android for long enoungh to say if a game is worth my time or someone else's time.

What I Do For The Website

This website delivers concise and straightforward reviews on various android games with varying genres. Each of my review applies a pragmatic and experience-based approach, such as design, gameplay mechanics, performance, user experience, and overall value, simply to name a few.

Why This Site Matters

Mobile gaming represents one of the most accessible and widely used platforms in the world. However, players often encounter reviews that are either overly promotional or sometimes disconnected from the actual experience of playing. By creating my own website and providing honest reviews, i could bridge some gaps by providing authentic, personal verdicts that respects the player’s time and attention.

My Independent Statement

All reviews published are based on my personal gameplay experience and may not reflect on everyone’s experience. I do not accept payment or promotional incentives from developers or publishers. My reviews reflect independent words and judgment and are written in good faith for the benefits of players who may need a second word before/after playing.
